## Lagrangian dynamics

<Equation
  label="Lagrangian"
  latex="L(q_i,\dot{q}_i,t)\equiv T-V"
  notes={true}
/>

* $T(q_i,\dot{q}_i,t)$ is the total kinetic energy
* $V(q_i,t)$ is the potential energy with only conservative forces
* $q_i$ and $\dot{q}_i$ are the generalized coordinates and velocities
* $i=1,\dotsc,n-k$, where $n$ is the number of degrees of freedom and $k$ is the number of holonomic constraints

<Equation
  label="Canonical momentum"
  latex="p_i\equiv\dfrac{\partial L}{\partial \dot{q}_i}"
/>

<Equation
  label="Lagrange's equations of motion"
  latex="\dfrac{\partial L}{\partial q_i} = \dfrac{d}{dt}\dfrac{\partial L}{\partial\dot{q}_i} = \dot{p}_i"
/>

<Equation
  label="Lagrange's equations of motion with undetermined multipliers"
  latex="\dfrac{d}{dt}\dfrac{\partial L}{\partial\dot{q}_i} - \dfrac{\partial L}{\partial q_i} = Q_i = \sum_k \lambda_k \dfrac{\partial f_k}{\partial q_i}"
/>

## Hamiltonian dynamics

<Equation
  label="Hamiltonian"
  latex="H(p_i,q_i,t)\equiv\sum_i p_i\dot{q}_i-L(q_i,\dot{q}_i,t)"
  notes={true}
/>

The Hamiltonian is a Legendre transform from $(q,\dot{q},t)$ to $(p,q,t)$ 

<Equation
  label="Hamilton's equations of motion"
  latex="\dfrac{\partial H}{\partial p_i}=\dot{q}_i\text{,}~~~~\dfrac{\partial H}{\partial q_i}=-\dot{p}_i"
/>

## Poisson brackets

<Equation

/>

## General equation of motion for a noninertial observer

<Equation
  label="General EoM"
  latex="m\ddot{\mathbf{r}} = \mathbf{F}_{\text{ext}} - m\ddot{\mathbf{s}} + \mathbf{F}_{\text{Cor}} + \mathbf{F}_{\text{cf}} + \mathbf{F}_{\alpha}"
  notes={true}
/>

EoM for a particle $m$ as seen by an observer fixed in the translating and rotating (body-fixed) frame.

* $\mathbf{F}_{\text{ext}} = m\ddot{\mathbf{r}}_0$ <Label>EXTERNAL FORCE</Label>
* $\mathbf{F}_{\text{Cor}}=-2m\boldsymbol{\omega}\times\dot{\mathbf{r}}$ <Label>Coriolis force</Label>
* $\mathbf{F}_{\text{cf}}=-m\boldsymbol{\omega}\times(\boldsymbol{\omega}\times\mathbf{r})$ <Label>Centrifugal force</Label>
* $\mathbf{F}_{\alpha}=-m\dot{\boldsymbol{\omega}}\times\mathbf{r}$ <Label>Euler force</Label>

## Approximate equation of motion on the surface of Earth

<Equation
  label="EoM on Earth"
  latex="m\ddot{\mathbf{r}}_{\text{E}} = \mathbf{F}_{\text{g}}+\mathbf{F}_{\text{Cor}}+\mathbf{F}_{\text{cf}}+{\mathbf{F}}'"
/>

<Equation
  label="Gravitational force"
  latex="\mathbf{F}_{\text{g}}=-GM_{\text{E}} m\hat{\mathbf{r}}/r^2"
/>

${\mathbf{F}}'$ represents additional terrestrial forces (e.g., a supporting string)

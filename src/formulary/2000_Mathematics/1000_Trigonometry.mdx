## Fundamental identities

1. $\tan\theta = \tfrac{\sin\theta}{\cos\theta}$
1. $\csc\theta = \tfrac{1}{\sin\theta}$
1. $\sec\theta = \tfrac{1}{\cos\theta}$
1. $\cot\theta = \tfrac{1}{\tan\theta}$
1. $\cos^2\theta+\sin^2\theta = 1$
1. $\csc^2\theta-\cot^2\theta=1$
1. $\sec^2\theta-\tan^2\theta=1$

## Double-angle and half-angle formulas

1. $\sin 2\theta = 2\sin\theta\cos\theta = \tfrac{2\tan\theta}{1+\tan^2\theta}$
1. $\cos 2\theta = \cos^2\theta-\sin^2\theta = 2\cos^2\theta-1 = 1-2\sin^2\theta = \tfrac{1-\tan^2\theta}{1+\tan^2\theta}$
1. $\tan 2\theta = \tfrac{2\tan\theta}{1-\tan^2\theta}$
1. $\sin\tfrac{\theta}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\tfrac{1-\cos\theta}{2}}$
1. $\sin^2\theta = \tfrac{1}{2}(1-\cos2\theta)$
1. $\cos\tfrac{\theta}{2} = \pm\sqrt{\tfrac{1+\cos\theta}{2}}$
1. $\cos^2\theta = \tfrac{1}{2}(1+\cos2\theta)$
1. $\tan\tfrac{\theta}{2} = \tfrac{\sin\theta}{1+\cos\theta} = \tfrac{1-\cos\theta}{\sin\theta} = \pm\sqrt{\tfrac{1-\cos\theta}{1+\cos\theta}} = \csc\theta -\cot\theta$

## Sum and difference formulas

1. $\sin(\alpha\pm\beta) = \sin\alpha\cos\beta \pm\cos\alpha\sin\beta $
1. $\cos(\alpha\pm\beta) = \cos\alpha\cos\beta \mp\sin\alpha\sin\beta $
1. $\tan(\alpha\pm\beta) = \tfrac{\tan\alpha\pm\tan\beta}{1\mp\tan\alpha\tan\beta}$

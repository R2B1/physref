## Linear vector spaces

<Equation
  label="Generalized vector represented in a specific basis"
  latex="|\alpha⟩ = \displaystyle\sum_{i=1}^n a_i |i⟩ = \begin{bmatrix} a_1 \\ a_2 \\ \vdots \\ a_n \end{bmatrix}"
  notes
/>

Here the vectors $|i⟩$ form a basis, which is a set of $n$ linearly independent vectors in an $n$-dimensional space, and $a_i$ are the components of the vector $|\alpha⟩$ in that basis.

<Equation
  label="Inner (or scalar or dot) product"
  latex="⟨\alpha|\beta⟩ = \begin{bmatrix} a_1^*, a_2^*, \cdots, a_n^* \end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} b_1 \\ b_2 \\ \vdots \\ b_n \end{bmatrix}"
/>
